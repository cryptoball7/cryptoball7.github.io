Frequently Asked Questions

What is a backflow prevention device?

A mechanical device that prevents contaminated water from flowing backwards into clean drinking water lines. It’s required on many commercial and irrigation systems.

How often should devices be tested?

Typically once per year, or as required by your local purveyor. We provide reminders so you never miss a deadline.

Do you submit reports for me?

Yes. After testing, we submit results directly to the water purveyor and email you copies for your records.

Do you repair failed devices?

Absolutely. Most repairs are completed during the same visit using OEM parts so you pass re‑inspection quickly.

Which areas do you serve?

San Bernardino, Riverside, Orange, Los Angeles, and San Diego counties. We also have a NorCal partner network — ask for coverage.
