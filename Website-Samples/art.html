About the Artist

Alex Rowan (b. 1990) explores the intersection of quiet moments and strong geometry. Working primarily with natural light, Alex composes images that prioritize negative space and tactile detail. The practice mixes analog and digital workflows; recent projects investigate abandoned domestic interiors and coastal morphology.
